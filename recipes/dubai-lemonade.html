Dubai Lemonade

Description

Whenever weâ€™ve traveled in the Middle East, in places like Jordan and Dubai, mint lemonade was on the menu at every restaurant.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 whole lemons, peeled (or 1/2 cup lemon juice)
  • 1 cup fresh mint
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup ice

Steps

  1. Measure out the sugar. Wash and peel your lemons and lime.
  2. Wash and pat dry the fresh mint.
  3. Add all ingredients to a blender (holding aside sliced lemon/lime and a few springs of mint for garnish) and blend on high for 1 minute.
  4. Pour, serve, and enjoy!
